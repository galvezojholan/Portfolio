What is Computer Science?

This work of computer scietists falls into three categories:

  • designing and building software
  • developing effective ways to solve computing problems, such as storing information in databases, sending data oer networks or providing new approaches to security problems.
  • devising new and better ways of using computers and addressing particular challenges in areas such as robotics, computer vision, or digital forensics

What is Computer Industry?

The 'Computer Industry' refers to the sector focused on the development, production, and distribution of computer products. It thrives on the constant expansion of computer applications across various sectors of society, aiming to generate economic returns and create favorable conditions for industry growth.

Key findings from recent PSA surveys, including the 2021 Survey on Information and Communications Technology (SICT), highlight these trends:

  • Growth In ICT utilization
    The Philippine computing industry shows strong growth in ICT use, with over half of employees using computers and internet, especially in ICT-enabled services (83.5% computer use) in 2021, though challenges like cost and infrastructure gaps persist, driving government and World Bank efforts to expand digital access and skills for greater inclusion and economic transformation, particularly for MSMEs and underserved areas.

  • Expansion of Mobile Transactions
    The number of firms increased significantly between 2017 and 2019. utilizing mobile phone purchases, increasing by 133.1%. This illustrates the growing dependence of company operations on mobile technology.

  • E-commerence and digital Engagement
    E-commerce usage has been increasing as more companies participate in with transactions conducted online. Additionally, social media presence has increased. with about half of businesses in a variety of sectors currently keeping up social media profiles

Computer Programmers Write, modify, and test code and scripts that allow software and applications to function properly. Bachelor's degree $98,670
Computer Support Specialists Maintain computer networks and provide technical help to users. Some computer knowledge $61,550
Computer Systems Analysts Analysts study an organization's current computer systems and design ways to improve efficiency. Bachelor's degree $103,790
Database Administrators and Architects Create or organize systems to store and secure data. Bachelor's degree $123,100
Information Security Analysts Plan and carry out security measures to protect an organization's computer networks and systems. Bachelor's degree $124,910
